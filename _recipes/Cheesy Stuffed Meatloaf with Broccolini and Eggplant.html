Cheesy Stuffed Meatloaf with Broccolini and Eggplant

Beef, Ground Beef, Meat

Prep Time: 20min Cook Time: 1h 5min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 6 portions Source: cookidoo.thermomix.com

Ingredients

Cheesy Stuffed Meatloaf

2 oz Parmesan cheese

3 garlic cloves

1 tbsp avocado oil

6 oz green pepper, quartered

5 oz red onions, quartered

½ tsp dried thyme

kosher salt, to taste

ground black pepper, to taste

32 oz lean ground beef (2 lb)

2 large eggs, organic, pasture-raised

5 oz provolone cheese, sliced

4 oz prosciutto, sliced (optional)

ketchup, keto (sugar free)

or 1 can tomato sauce (8 oz)

Broccolini and Eggplant

3 garlic cloves

2 ½ oz shallots, thinly sliced

1 tsp mustard seeds

2 ½ oz avocado oil

or 2 ½ oz extra virgin olive oil

8 oz eggplant, cubed (1 in.)

16 oz broccolini, cut into pieces (2 in.)

kosher salt, to taste

ground black pepper, to taste

Directions

Cheesy Stuffed Meatloaf

Preheat oven to 325°F. Line a loaf pan (9 in. x 5 in.) with parchment, letting edges of paper hang over two sides of pan for easy removal. Line a baking sheet with parchment and set aside prepared pans.

Place Parmesan cheese into mixing bowl and grate 6 sec/speed 7. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.

Add avocado oil, green pepper, onion, thyme, salt and pepper and chop 3 sec/speed 5. Without measuring cup, cook 8 min/250°F/speed 1.

Transfer cooked mixture to a large bowl, add ground beef, reserved grated Parmesan and eggs and mix well to combine using hands or spatula. Place a piece of parchment (9 in. x 10 in.) onto a clean work surface. Transfer beef mixture to parchment and spread out to create a rectangular shape to fit the paper. Layer provolone and prosciutto so they overlap on the meatloaf.

Roll up meat with aid of parchment to create a loaf shape, pinching seams and edges of meat to seal. Remove parchment from meat and place rolled meatloaf into prepared loaf pan, seam side down. Cover meatloaf with as much keto ketchup as desired and bake 75-80 minutes (325°F) until meat thermometer registers an internal temperature of 155°F. Meanwhile, clean mixing bowl. During the last 20 minutes of baking prepare broccolini and eggplant.

Broccolini and Eggplant

Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 6 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.

Add shallots, mustard seeds and avocado oil and without measuring cup cook 20 min/250°F//speed 1. Place eggplant and broccolini onto prepared baking sheet, toss with onion-garlic mixture, sprinkle with salt and pepper and set aside. Remove meatloaf from oven, drain fat if needed, tent with foil and allow to cool for 10-20 minutes while baking vegetables.

Increase oven temperature to 425°F. Place reserved baking sheet with seasoned vegetables into oven and bake 10-20 minutes (425°F) until edges of eggplant are golden. Set aside to cool. Serve Cheesy Stuffed Meatloaf with Broccolini and Eggplant.
