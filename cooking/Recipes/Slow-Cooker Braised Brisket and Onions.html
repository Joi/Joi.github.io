Slow-Cooker Braised Brisket and Onions

Beef, Brisket

Cook Time: 9 to 10 hours on low or 6 to 7 hours on high Servings: Serves 10 to 12 Source: americastestkitchen.com

Ingredients

GATHER YOUR INGREDIENTS

1 tablespoon paprika

2 teaspoons onion powder

Salt and pepper

1 teaspoon garlic powder

⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper

1 (5-pound) beef brisket, flat cut, fat trimmed to 1/4 inch

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

3 onions, halved and sliced 1/2 inch thick

3 tablespoons all-purpose flour

1 tablespoon tomato paste

3 garlic cloves, minced

1 cup chicken broth

2 tablespoons plus 1 teaspoon red wine vinegar

1 tablespoon packed brown sugar

3 sprigs fresh thyme

3 bay leaves

Directions

Combine paprika, onion powder, 1 teaspoon salt, garlic powder, and cayenne in bowl. Using fork, prick brisket all over. Rub spice mixture over brisket, wrap tightly in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for 8 to 24 hours.

Heat oil in 12-inch skillet over medium heat until shimmering. Add onions and cook until softened and lightly browned, 8 to 10 minutes. Stir in flour, tomato paste, and garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Slowly stir in broth, scraping up any browned bits and smoothing out any lumps; transfer to slow cooker.

Stir 2 tablespoons vinegar, sugar, thyme sprigs, and bay leaves into slow cooker. Unwrap brisket and nestle fat side up into slow cooker. Spoon portion of onion mixture over brisket. Cover and cook until beef is tender, 9 to 10 hours on low or 6 to 7 hours on high.

Transfer brisket to carving board, tent loosely with aluminum foil, and let rest for 20 minutes.

Discard thyme sprigs and bay leaves. Using large spoon, skim fat from surface of sauce. Stir in remaining 1 teaspoon vinegar and season with salt and pepper to taste. Slice brisket against grain into ½-inch-thick slices and arrange on serving dish. Spoon 1 cup sauce over brisket and serve, passing remaining sauce separately.
