Sausage and Veggie Frittata and Ayran Drink

★★★★

Egg, Italian

Prep Time: 15min Cook Time: 40min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 8 pieces Source: cookidoo.thermomix.com

Ingredients

Ayran Drink

9 oz plain Greek yogurt, whole fat

9 oz cold water, filtered

¼ tsp salt

Frittata

1 oz fresh dill, leaves only

1 oz fresh parsley, leaves only, plus extra to garnish

3 oz Pecorino cheese, cubed

4 oz red onion, in pieces

6 oz broccoli florets

½ oz avocado oil, plus 1 tbsp, to fry

6 oz zucchini, in pieces

1 heaping tsp smoked paprika

1 tsp ground cumin

1 tsp dried oregano

½ tsp ground cayenne pepper

1 tsp salt, divided

¼ tsp cracked black pepper, divided

8 oz ground lamb

6 oz pear tomatoes, halved

12 large eggs, organic, pasture-raised

Directions

Ayran Drink

Place yogurt, water and salt into mixing bowl and blend 15 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a pitcher and refrigerate until ready to serve. Clean mixing bowl.

Frittata

Preheat oven to 400°F.

Place dill and parsley into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 8. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.

Place Pecorino cheese into mixing bowl and grate 7 sec/speed 9. Transfer to a separate bowl and set aside.

Place onion, broccoli and 0.5 oz avocado oil into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 4. Add zucchini, paprika, cumin, oregano, cayenne, ½ tsp salt and ⅛ tsp pepper and without measuring cup cook 5 min/250°F//speed 1. Meanwhile, heat 1 tbsp avocado oil in a large cast iron skillet over medium-high heat. Once hot, add lamb and cook, breaking up with spatula until well browned and almost cooked through (approx. 5-7 min.).

Drain off excess fat from browned lamb. Add reserved parsley-dill mixture, tomatoes and cooked veggies from mixing bowl to skillet with lamb.

Place eggs, ½ tsp salt and ⅛ tsp pepper into mixing bowl and mix 30 sec/speed 8. Pour egg mixture into skillet with browned lamb and vegetables and stir over medium heat just until egg begins to set (approx. 7-10 min.). Transfer skillet to oven and bake 10-15 minutes (400°F) or until center is set. Remove skillet from oven. Loosen edges with a spatula, transfer to a serving platter, cut into 8 pieces and serve.
