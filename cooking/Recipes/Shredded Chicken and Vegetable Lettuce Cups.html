Shredded Chicken and Vegetable Lettuce Cups

Chicken

Prep Time: 1h Cook Time: 1h 15min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4 portions Source: cookidoo.thermomix.com

Ingredients

1 garlic clove

1 piece fresh ginger (approx. 1 in. x ¾ in.)

2 oz carrot, cut into 2 in. pieces

2 oz celery, cut into 2 in. pieces

16 - 18 oz chicken breast, boneless and skinless, cubed (approx. 1 in.)

1 oz green asparagus tips (approx. ½ bunch) (optional)

2 oz red pepper, diced

½ stick fresh lemongrass (approx. ½ in. x 3 in.), thinly sliced

2 fresh kaffir lime leaves

2 oz frozen sweet corn

2 tbsp fish sauce

2 tbsp tamari sauce

1 tbsp sesame oil

2 oz water

1 serrano chili, to taste, finely sliced into rounds (optional)

3 oz green onions, cut on the bias (approx. ¼ in.)

2 oz canned water chestnuts, drained and diced

1 oz cilantro, leaves and tender stems, finely chopped (approx. ½ bunch)

20 mint leaves, torn into small pieces

½ oz lime juice (approx. ½ lime)

27 oz iceberg lettuce, cored, wash and separate leaves, to serve

2 tbsp white sesame seeds, toasted

Directions

Place garlic and ginger into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 7.

Add carrot and celery and chop 4 sec/speed 5. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.

Add chicken, asparagus tips, red pepper, lemongrass, lime leaves, corn, fish sauce, tamari, sesame oil and water and cook 10 min/250°F//speed.

Add chili, green onions, water chestnuts, cilantro, mint leaves and lime juice and mix 20 sec//speed .

Place simmering basket into mixing bowl, hold in place with spatula and drain liquid from chicken and vegetables into a bowl and reserve for future use. Discard lime leaves and transfer chicken mixture to a serving bowl. Serve in crispy lettuce cups sprinkled with sesame seeds.

Tips & tricks

Toast sesame seeds before starting recipe. Place 1 oz sesame seeds into mixing bowl and without measuring cup toast 10 min/Varoma/speed .

If you prefer a slightly saltier version, add 2 tablespoons fish sauce.

Keep drained liquid and combine with water when steaming rice, fish or chicken in the Varoma.
