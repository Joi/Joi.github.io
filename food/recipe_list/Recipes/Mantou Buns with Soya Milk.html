Mantou Buns with Soya Milk

★★★★

Bread and Biscuits

Prep Time: 15min Cook Time: 1h 35min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 12 portions Source: cookidoo.thermomix.com

Ingredients

7 oz soybeans

warm water, to soak

Mantou Buns

1 ½ tsp active dry yeast

1 ½ oz brown sugar

8 ½ oz water

18 oz all-purpose flour, plus extra to dust

2 tsp vegetable oil, plus extra to grease

1 tsp salt

Soya Milk

41 oz cold water

2 - 3 oz sugar, to taste

Directions

Place simmering basket onto mixing bowl lid and weigh in soybeans. Remove simmering basket with soybeans and submerge in a bowl of warm water. Leave to soak for at least one hour or overnight.

Mantou Buns

Place yeast, brown sugar and water into mixing bowl and warm 1 min 30 sec/100°F/speed 1.

Add flour, oil and salt and mix 5 sec/speed 5. Knead Dough /3 min. Meanwhile, lightly grease Varoma dish and tray.

Transfer dough to a lightly floured work surface. Cover and let rest 15 minutes. Roll dough into a rectangle (18 in. x 8 in.). Starting from the longest side, roll the dough tightly towards the other end to form a log. Cut the log into 12 equal pieces. Divide rolls, seam side down between Varoma dish and tray and set aside to proof (20 min.) while prepping the Soya Milk.

Soya Milk

Rinse soaked soybeans under cold running water. Place rinsed soybeans and water into mixing bowl, set Varoma with Mantou Buns into position, secure Varoma lid and steam 30 min/212°F//speed 2. Remove Varoma with steamed buns and set aside.

Add sugar and start Blend /3 min. Serve Soya Milk with Mantou Buns or store in an airtight container and refrigerate.

Tips & tricks

Steam half the dough on the Varoma tray, refrigerate remaining dough and steam as needed.

For an oat flavored soya milk: after blending, add 1.5 oz oats and cook for an additional 7 min/212°F/speed 2. Blend 3 min/speed 7. Serve warm or store in an airtight container and refrigerate.
