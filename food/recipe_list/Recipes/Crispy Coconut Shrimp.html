Crispy Coconut Shrimp

★★★★★

Shrimp

Prep Time: 10min Cook Time: 20min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4 portions Source: cookidoo.thermomix.com

Ingredients

Sriracha Dipping Sauce

1 garlic clove

4 oz mayonnaise, sugar-free

1 lime, zested and juiced

2 - 3 tbsp sambal oelek

¼ tsp salt (optional)

Coconut Shrimp

2 oz pork rinds

2 oz unsweetened coconut flakes

2 ½ oz coconut flour

1 tsp garlic powder

1 tsp onion powder

2 large eggs, organic, pasture-raised

16 oz large raw shrimp, peeled, deveined, tail on

avocado oil, to fry

Directions

Sriracha Dipping Sauce

Place garlic into mixing bowl and chop 3 sec/speed 10. Scrape down sides of mixing bowl with spatula.

Add mayonnaise, lime zest and juice, sambal oelek and salt and mix 15 sec/speed 3. Transfer to a bowl and set aside. Clean and dry mixing bowl.

Coconut Shrimp

Place pork rinds into mixing bowl and grind 10 sec/speed 10.

Add coconut flakes and grind 5 sec/speed 10. Transfer to a shallow bowl. Place coconut flour, garlic powder and onion powder into a second shallow bowl.

Place eggs into mixing bowl and mix 10 sec/speed 5. Transfer to a third shallow bowl. Place a wire rack over a baking sheet.

Pat shrimp dry with paper towel and dredge each shrimp into coconut flour mixture, shake off excess. Next, dip into egg mixture and finally the pork rind mixture, pressing gently to coat. Transfer coated shrimp to prepared wire rack and allow to dry (approx. 15 min.).

Heat 1 inch of oil in a large frying pan to 350°F. Working in batches, fry shrimp until golden brown (approx. 2-3 min. per side) using tongs for turning. Transfer fried shrimp to paper towels to dry and continue until all shrimp are fried.

Serve Crispy Coconut Shrimp with Sriracha Dipping Sauce.
