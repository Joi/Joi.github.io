Guacamole

★★★

Spread

Prep Time: 5min Cook Time: 5min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 1 total recipe Source: cookidoo.thermomix.com

Ingredients

3 ½ oz onions, halved

1 serrano chili, deseeded

½ - 1 oz lime juice

½ - 1 tsp salt, to taste

2 - 3 sprigs fresh cilantro, leaves only

7 oz tomatoes, quartered

18 oz ripe avocado, cut into pieces (2-3 avocados)

Directions

Place onions, chili, lime juice, salt and cilantro into mixing bowl and chop 5 sec/speed 5.

Add tomatoes and chop 3 sec/speed 5.

Add avocado and mix 5-10 sec//speed 5. Adjust seasoning to taste. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to serve.

Tips & tricks

Serve with tortilla chips or crackers.

Fresh chilies come in different strengths. Removing the seeds will tone down the spiciness.

Adjust mixing time of avocados according to their ripeness and desired guacamole texture.

To prevent browning, garnish the guacamole with an avocado pit, tomato slices, or cover with cling wrap in direct contact with the guacamole surface.

Replace fresh chili with ¼-1 tsp cayenne pepper, to taste.

Add hot sauce to taste.
