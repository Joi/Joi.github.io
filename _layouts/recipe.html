Recipe Copyright

According to the US Copyright Office, "A mere listing of ingredients is not protected under copyright law. However, where a recipe or formula is accompanied by substantial literary expression in the form of an explanation or directions, or when there is a collection of recipes as in a cookbook, there may be a basis for copyright protection." On this site, for recipes written by others, the ingredients are listed and a link to the original recipe has been provided.
