#### Volume Equivalencies

* 3 tsp = 1 tbsp
* 2 tbsp = 1 fl. oz
* 16 tbsp = 1 cup (8 fl. oz)
* 2 cups = 1 pint (16 fl. oz)
* 4 cups = 1 qt (32 fl. oz)
* 1 qt = 0.95 l
* 4 qt = 1 g
